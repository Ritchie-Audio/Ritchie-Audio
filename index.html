Hello everyone

My name is Ritchie Young. I am an experienced sound engineer, digital transformation coordinator and a freelancer based in Perth, Scotland.

I am always looking for freelance work opportunities. Get in touch with me via my social links or email address in my CV for any queries you may have.

Live Sound

I have expeience in working both in the UK and internationally. Most sound brands and technologies covered including: Yamaha, Digico, Shure RF, Sennheiser RF, Dante, Symetrix, Qlab & Smaart.

Digital

Recently I have been working as the Digital Transformation Coordinator in Perth museum where I have been introducing digital activities and running training sessions aimed at upskilling the workforce.

Exhibition Builds

I recently designed a touchless interactive map for Perth Museum. The map was georeferenced and showed points of interest spanning the Roman period in Perthshire. The design included the 3D print of the physical model & the animation and textures projected.

Theatre Sound

Since 2010 I have been working mostly as a theatre sound engineer and have worked both as a freelancer and in house.

My experience has taken me from Scotland to all over the world whilst operating shows in tiny little event halls to national theatres. I find the creation of a new work exciting and the process fulfilling.

Most recently I was the Sound Supervisor for The Stamping Ground at Eden Court in Inverness, The Comedy of Errors tour with the Citizens Theatre and James IV tour with Raw Material Arts.

Heritage Digital

I love introducing digital elements into exhibitions. Trying to find the best and most relevant way of engaging the visitors is super challenging but a wonderful land to explore. There is so much scope.

I spent quite a long time adapting & creating 3D content at Culture Perth and Kinross and have built and implemented a series of tools which aide the creation of 3D assets. This has resulted in a rendering process which has been reduced from 8+ hours to 30 minutes.
